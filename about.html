About Us

Bondi & Bourke Davao offers a refined yet relaxed dining experience in the heart of the city, inspired by the bold flavors of modern Australian cuisine. The restaurant includes a main dining area, alfresco bar, private rooms, and a boutique hotel, making it perfect for everything from casual drinks to special celebrations. At Bondi & Bourke, every visit is about great food, warm service, and memorable experiences.

History

Bondi & Bourke was founded by Australian Chef Wade Watson, who brought his culinary expertise to the Philippines. The restaurant’s name reflects a mix of iconic Australian influences: Bondi, representing the relaxed, coastal lifestyle of Bondi Beach, and Bourke, referring to Melbourne’s Bourke Street, known for its vibrant food and cultural scene. Together, the name captures a balance of laid-back beachside charm and sophisticated city dining, showcasing the diversity of modern Australian cuisine.
