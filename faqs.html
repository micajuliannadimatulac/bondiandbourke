Frequently Asked Questions

What type of cuisine does Bondi & Bourke serve?

Bondi & Bourke specializes in modern Australian cuisine, offering a mix of Western and Filipino favorites using premium ingredients.

Do I need a reservation before dining at Bondi & Bourke?

Reservations are highly recommended, especially for dinner and weekends, as the restaurant can get busy. Walk-ins are accepted depending on table availability.

Can I host private events or celebrations at Bondi & Bourke?

Yes, Bondi & Bourke offers private dining options for events, parties, or business meetings. Contact the restaurant directly for packages and availability.

Is parking available at Bondi & Bourke?

Yes, Bondi & Bourke offers nearby parking options. Some may have limited in-house parking or access to building/valet services.

Does Bondi & Bourke accommodate food allergies?

Yes, the staff can accommodate food allergies or dietary restrictions. It’s best to inform your server in advance so adjustments can be made.

Is there a corkage fee at Bondi & Bourke?

Yes, a corkage fee typically applies if you bring your own wine or cake. You may contact the restaurant directly for current rates.
